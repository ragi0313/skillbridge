SkillBridge Credit Withdrawal System Documentation
Overview
The SkillBridge platform allows mentors and learners to withdraw their earned credits and convert them back to real money. This system ensures secure, transparent, and efficient processing of credit-to-cash conversions.
Basic Conversion Rate

5 credits = $1 USD
Example: 100 credits = $20 USD

How the Withdrawal System Works
1. User Requirements
Before users can withdraw credits, they must:

Complete payment account setup through Stripe
Verify their identity and banking information
Have earned or purchased credits in their account
Meet minimum withdrawal requirements

2. Minimum Withdrawal Amounts

Standard minimum: 25 credits ($5 USD)
This minimum helps reduce transaction costs and processing overhead
Users can accumulate more credits before withdrawing to maximize their net payout

3. Withdrawal Fees
We implement a fair fee structure to cover payment processing and platform maintenance:
Fee Structure: 1.5% with $0.50 minimum fee

Small withdrawals: $0.50 flat fee (to keep processing viable)
Larger withdrawals: 1.5% of the total amount
Maximum fee cap: Never more than 5% of withdrawal amount

Fee Examples:

$5 withdrawal → $0.50 fee → You receive $4.50
$20 withdrawal → $0.50 fee → You receive $19.50
$50 withdrawal → $0.75 fee → You receive $49.25
$100 withdrawal → $1.50 fee → You receive $98.50

4. Payment Processing

Payment Partner: Stripe (industry-leading security and reliability)
Account Type: Stripe Express accounts for users
Processing Time: Instant to your Stripe account, 1-3 business days to your bank
Supported Methods: Bank transfers, debit cards (depending on your region)

Step-by-Step Withdrawal Process
For First-Time Users:

Setup Payment Account

Click "Withdraw Credits" in your dashboard
Complete Stripe account setup (identity verification required)
Connect your bank account or debit card
Verify account information



For Regular Withdrawals:

Request Withdrawal

Go to your wallet/credits section
Enter the number of credits you want to withdraw
Review the fee breakdown and net amount you'll receive
Confirm the withdrawal request


Processing

Credits are immediately deducted from your account
Payment is processed through Stripe
You receive confirmation email with transaction details


Receiving Payment

Funds appear in your Stripe account instantly
Money transfers to your bank account within 1-3 business days
You can track status in your withdrawal history

Withdrawal Policies
Processing Schedule:

Manual Withdrawals Only: Users control when and how much to withdraw
Processing Hours: 24/7 automated processing
Business Days: Bank transfers process Monday-Friday

Limits and Restrictions:

Daily Limit: $500 per day (can be increased for verified premium users)
Monthly Limit: $2,000 per month for standard users
Failed Transactions: Credits returned to account if withdrawal fails

Special Circumstances:

Disputed Earnings: Credits from disputed transactions may be held
Platform Violations: Withdrawal privileges may be suspended
Account Closure: Final withdrawal processed within 30 days

Fee Transparency
Why We Charge Fees:

Payment Processing Costs: Stripe charges 0.25% for transfers
Platform Maintenance: System upkeep and security costs
Customer Support: Handling withdrawal-related inquiries
Compliance Costs: Meeting financial regulations

Fee Optimization Tips:

Withdraw Larger Amounts: Higher amounts have lower percentage fees
Plan Your Withdrawals: Avoid frequent small withdrawals
Premium Users: May qualify for reduced fees or free withdrawal quotas

Troubleshooting Common Issues
Setup Problems:

Identity Verification Failed: Check document quality and information accuracy
Bank Account Issues: Ensure account details are correct and account is active
Unsupported Region: Contact support for alternative payment methods

Processing Issues:

Withdrawal Pending: Normal processing time is 1-3 business days
Failed Transfer: Check bank account status, credits automatically returned
Missing Funds: Contact support with transaction ID for investigation

Account Issues:

Insufficient Credits: Ensure you have enough credits after fees
Withdrawal Disabled: May be temporary due to account verification needs
Technical Errors: Platform support available 24/7
